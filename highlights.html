highlights

ROB501: Computer Vision for Robotics Hackathon - Pano-Mania! Oct 2022 highlights Hackathon focused on reconstructing a panoramic image. Participants applied feature detection, homography computation (with RANSAC), bilinear interpolation, and heuristic viegnetting correction to complete the challenge. I acted as one of the organizers and provided additional support to participants. U of T AI Summit Roundtable with the President of South Korea Yoon Suk-yeol Sept 2022 highlights The purpose was to discuss AI - it's rise, potential applications, and opportunities for further collaboration between U of T and South Korean partners. I acted as the tech lead and one of the representatives on behalf of U of T's Robotics Institute. Manipulation, Automation, and Robotics at Small Scale (MARSS) Conference July 2022 highlights MARSS is an international conference for cross-disciplinary R&D communities to discuss manipulation & automation (at micro/nano scales), and all kinds of small scale robotics. I was a part of the organization comittee and acted as the volunteer coordinator. AI · CRV Conference May - June 2022 highlights AI·CRV is an international conference focused on Canadian research for artificial intelligence, robots, and computer vision. I acted as a co-host and admin for the AI section of the conference.
